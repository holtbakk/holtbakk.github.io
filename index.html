Configuration Notes
ℹ VLAN 10 Core — Infrastructure Isolation
VLAN 10 is reserved exclusively for network infrastructure: Firewalla Gold SE, Zyxel XMG1915-10EP, and Firewalla AP7. Only VLAN 20 Trusted is permitted to reach this segment for management. Core is not advertised as a wireless SSID — the AP7 trunk on port 8 carries VLANs 20–60 only.
⚠ mDNS Relay (required)
Sonos apps (on VLAN 20 / 30) must discover Sonos speakers (VLAN 60) across VLAN boundaries. WiiM Mini control apps similarly need cross-VLAN mDNS. Enable the mDNS Relay on Firewalla Gold SE and include VLANs 20, 30, and 60. Without this, Sonos and WiiM devices will not be discoverable from Trusted or Kids networks.
⚠ IGMP Multicast — Altibox PVR (VLAN 60)
Altibox uses IGMP multicast for live TV. After moving the PVR to VLAN 60, verify that IGMP snooping is correctly configured on the Zyxel switch and that Firewalla handles multicast routing for VLAN 60. Test live TV immediately after migration.
ℹ USB Wi-Fi SD Failover
The Firewalla Gold SE has a USB Wi-Fi SD adapter configured as a secondary WAN. If the Altibox fiber connection drops, 5G sharing Wi-Fi from phone will handle WAN. VLAN segmentation and firewall rules remain active during failover.
ℹ Static IP Reservations
Infrastructure nodes use static IPs on VLAN 10 Core for stable firewall rules and logging:
Zyxel XMG1915-10EP: 10.10.10.2 · Firewalla AP7: 10.10.10.3
